Work

Kevin's Adventures - A Unity Project

A 2D Zelda like game, made in unity with a group of four in the timespan of 8 weeks. I've created all Enemy Scripts and AI, as well as the Health System, Attack and Death Scripts and Health Bar. To try the game, click here for the GitHub Repository.

VFX Graphs, Shaders and Mesh Generation in Unity

A project in cooperation with Liz Kintzel, to create VFX Graphs, Shaders and Mesh generated objects, partially with attached physics to them. My personal work involved the fireworks and "magic ball" VFX Graphs, the Water and Dissolve Shader and the Klein Bottle, Torus and Flowerball, as well as the Flowerballs physics. For a GitHub Repository, click here.

Graphlands - A Phaser based Game

In my studies I've been part of the project "Graphlands". To create a game based on the "Dollar Game", me and six other students came together to create an approachable riddle game for young and old. For more information on our achievements and game, you can visit the official IMI Showtime Wesite and learn more about the process and features.

About

Right after my Abitur (equivalent to A Levels), I have started my studies of International Media and Computing at the University for Applied Science in Berlin in October 2019. Since then I've been improving my skills in Web Technologies, Visual Computing, and most importantly Game Technologies & Interactive Systems.

I've always had a great interest in the Gaming Industry, especially the Technical Artist and Game Development roles. Therefore I began my specialisation in "Game Technology and Interactive Systems" during my studies, where I acquired knowledge in multiple programming languages and software programs. I especially enjoyed working with the Unity Engine, both for the creation of small games as well as VFX effects. In my self study I teamed up with a friend of mine to gain experience with the Unreal Engine and further my knowledge of Game Engines. In addition I learned to use the software Maya in my studies, where I created multiple models and textures for them.

Through some of my other projects, I've acquired extensive knowledge in Java and JavaScript, and have not only created some games in them, but also a multitude of other programs like image processing programs.
